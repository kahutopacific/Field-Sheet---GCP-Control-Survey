      📄 Save as PDF on iPhone
      
    

    

      

        Safari on iPhone doesn't support direct printing, but you can save a PDF in a few taps:
      


      
      

        
1

        

          
Tap the Share button

          
The □↑ icon at the bottom of your Safari screen (or top-right on iPad).

        

      


      
      

        
2

        

          
Scroll down and tap "Print"

          
In the share sheet, scroll down until you see the Print option and tap it.

        

      


      
      

        
3

        

          
Pinch open the page preview

          
In the print preview, pinch outward (zoom gesture) on the page thumbnail. This converts it to a PDF.

        

      


      
      

        
4

        

          
Tap Share □↑ → Save to Files

          
A new share sheet appears. Tap Save to Files to save the PDF to your iPhone, or share it via email / WhatsApp.

        

      


      

        💡 Tip: Photos you've added will appear in the PDF — 2 per row with captions. Make sure all sections are expanded before saving, otherwise collapsed sections won't print.
      


      

        
        
      

    

  







  

    

      📄 Save as PDF on Android
      
    

    

      

        Chrome on Android has a built-in Save to PDF feature. Follow these steps:
      


      
      

        
1

        

          
Tap the ⋮ menu (top right)

          
The three-dot menu in the top-right corner of Chrome.

        

      


      
      

        
2

        

          
Tap "Share"

          
Then select Print from the share options.

        

      


      
      

        
3

        

          
Select "Save as PDF" as the printer

          
In the print dialog, tap the printer name at the top and choose Save as PDF.

        

      


      
      

        
4

        

          
Tap the Download / Save button

          
The PDF saves to your Downloads folder. You can then share it via email, WhatsApp, or Google Drive.

        

      


      

        💡 Tip: Photos will appear in the PDF — 2 per row with captions. Make sure all sections are expanded before saving.
